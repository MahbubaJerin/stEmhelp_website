About stEmhelp

Engineering-oriented support and activities for Schools

stEmhelp was created with a primary focus on addressing the gap in engineering education within STEM programs. While Science, Technology, and Mathematics receive fair treatment in schools, the "E"ngineering component is often limited to basic robotics and Lego construction activities.

Why Engineering Activities Matter: Engineering activities naturally incorporate all STEM components - Science and Mathematics underpin Engineering principles, while Technology is developed by Engineers. This makes engineering-focused activities the perfect vehicle for comprehensive STEM education.

Our Mission: We provide K-12 teachers and students with hands-on experiment kits, educational resources, and training materials specifically designed to bring engineering concepts to life in the classroom. Our platform bridges the knowledge gap by offering both teacher training and student-focused learning materials.

The Problem We Solve: Many teachers lack understanding and knowledge of engineering concepts themselves, leading to limited engineering activities in schools. stEmhelp provides the resources needed to support both Primary and High Schools in delivering effective STEM education with a strong engineering focus.

Our Approach: Through interactive experiments, detailed tutorials, and comprehensive teaching modules, we make engineering concepts accessible and engaging for high school students while empowering educators with the tools they need to succeed.
